BECOMING LOLA MAE'S

Lola Mae's is a locally owned boutique based out of Medina, TN. We strive to meet your clothes shopping needs and bring you unique, chic styles at affordable prices.

Many have asked where our company name came from. As we decided to open our company, the owner, Brittlee, wanted a name that had a special meaning behind it. Lola Mae is her puppy that is her world. With a meaning so special to her heart, Lola Mae's Boutique has become a passion of hers to provide clothing for all women. We want to create a fun experience that every customer loves and enjoys. Whether you are a first-time shopper or one of our most loyal customers, we want you to know you are appreciated more than you know.
